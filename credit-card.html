Online Banking Services

To apply for the POSB Everyday Card:

Log into your digibank app, select 'More', then 'Deposit Account', and choose 'POSB Everyday Card'.

 

You can open your account any day of the week, including public holidays, from 7am to 10:30pm. On the last day of the month, account opening is available from 7am to 8pm. If you apply outside these hours, it will take 2 working days for your account to be set up.
